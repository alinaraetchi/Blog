HELLO FASHION

When it comes to putting together a winning fit that you’ll love (and probably draw tons of compliments on), stick to this simple formula: Staple + Staple + Statement/Showstopper + Accessories.

No outfit is complete without accessories. I usually lean towards 5 pieces or so – a bag, glasses/sunglasses, and a few pieces of jewelry. I have been wanting a crossbody, lightweight hands-free bag for when I’m out running errands with the kids and love this one. I like that it holds a little more than a fanny pack but still lightweight. I Each addition adds more interest and makes your look feel more intentional and finished.

Someone once told me that you can spot a vintage style blogger from a mile off. Spot a camera and you’ve already reached phase one; unless they’re a roaming photographer or an art student, they are almost definitely a blogger. Flat shoes is the second giveaway. And we’re not talking about any old flats either. We’re talking Chanel brogues or some sort of vintage burgundy croc loafers with a low to mid heel. Because the style blogger is all about comfort and style (how else do you think they get to and from events/meetings all day without getting blisters?).

A straw hat is a summer staple in my wardrobe. Not only does it protect you against the sun and helps to keep you cool, but it also elevates any summer outfit. I also love to reach for hats year-round when my hair is due for a shampoo or I’m not having a good hair day. Win-Win!
